# Marketing Campaign Performance Analysis (Power BI)

## Project Overview
This project analyzes a marketing campaign dataset to evaluate campaign effectiveness, customer behavior, and revenue performance. The goal is to transform raw campaign data into actionable business insights using Power BI.

The dashboard focuses on identifying high-performing campaigns, understanding customer response patterns, and uncovering opportunities for optimization.

---

## Business Objectives
- Evaluate overall campaign performance
- Identify top-performing and underperforming campaigns
- Analyze customer engagement and response rates
- Understand revenue contribution across campaigns and segments
- Support data-driven marketing decisions

---

## Dataset Description
The dataset contains marketing campaign records including:
- Customer demographics
- Campaign channels
- Response indicators
- Revenue and cost-related fields
- Time-based attributes

Raw data was cleaned and transformed before visualization.

---

## Data Preparation
The following steps were performed:
- Removed duplicates and invalid records
- Handled missing values
- Created calculated columns and measures
- Standardized categorical fields
- Built relationships between tables where applicable

---

## Key Metrics and KPIs
- Total Campaign Revenue
- Response Rate
- Conversion Rate
- Cost vs Revenue Performance
- Campaign Effectiveness by Channel
- Customer Segmentation Metrics

---

## Dashboard Pages
### 1. Overview
- High-level KPIs
- Campaign performance summary
- Revenue and response trends

### 2. Campaign Performance
- Performance by campaign type
- Channel comparison
- Revenue vs cost analysis

### 3. Insights & Recommendations
- Best-performing campaigns
- Areas of inefficiency
- Actionable recommendations for optimization

---

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- DAX
- Power Query
- Microsoft Excel (for initial data inspection)

---

## Key Insights
- Certain campaign channels consistently outperform others
- A small subset of campaigns contributes the majority of revenue
- Customer response varies significantly by segment
- Optimization opportunities exist in underperforming campaigns
